Definition

The repitition of the same letter or sound at the begginning of adjacent or closely connected words.

Examples

"She sold sea shells at the sea shore."
"I have heard how hedgehogs hog the hedge."
"Hungry hippos hurry past hungrier hikers."

Definition

A brief and indirect reference to a person, place, thing, or idea, of historical, cultural, literary, or political significance.

Examples

"Don't act like a Scrooge!"
"He studies all the time and is a regular Einstein!"
"My nutsack is my Achilles' Heel!"

Definition

A comparison between 2 things, typically on the basis of their structure and for the purpose of explaination or clarification.

Examples

"Finding a reason to care is like finding a hay in a needlestack."
"Girls are like Einsteins when it comes to finding friends."
"Tiktok is like a drug for teenagers."

Definition

A mild or indirect word or expression substituted for one considered to be too harsh or blunt when referring to something unpleasant or embarrassing.

Examples
