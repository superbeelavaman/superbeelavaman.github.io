Definition

The repitition of the same letter or sound at the begginning of adjacent or closely connected words.

Examples

"She sold sea shells at the sea shore."
"I have heard how hedgehogs hog the hedge."
"Hungry hippos hurry past hungrier hikers."

Definition

A brief and indirect reference to a person, place, thing, or idea, of historical, cultural, literary, or political significance.

Examples

"Don't act like a Scrooge!"
"He studies all the time and is a regular Einstein!"
"My nutsack is my Achilles' Heel!"

Definition

A comparison between 2 things, typically on the basis of their structure and for the purpose of explaination or clarification.

Examples

"Finding a reason to care is like finding a hay in a needlestack."
"Girls are like Einsteins when it comes to finding friends."
"Tiktok is like a drug for teenagers."

Definition

A mild or indirect word or expression substituted for one considered to be too harsh or blunt when referring to something unpleasant or embarrassing.

Examples

"You're Fired!" --> "Sadly, I must let you go."
"Have sex." --> "Make love."

Definition

Exaggerated statements or claims not meant to be taken literally.

Examples

"I could eat a horse!"
"This homework is going to take years!"
"This is the best cookie in the world!"

Definition

An expression used by a particular group of people with a meaning that is only known through common use.

Examples

"Make an extra effort." --> "Go the extra mile."
"To fail spectacularly." --> "To go down in flames."
"Feeling nervous." --> "Butterflies in my stomach."

Definition

Visually descriptive or figurative language, especially in a literary work.

Examples

"The cat was as smooth as silk."
"The new lights were as bright as the sun."
"The birds' singing was beautiful."

Definition

The use of words to convey the opposite of their literal meaning; a literary device in which contradictory statements or situations reveal a reality that is different from what appears to be true.

Examples

"The police station was robbed last night."
"The dog destroyed a dog training manual."

Definition

A figure of speech in which a word or phrase is applied to an object or action to which it is not literally acceptable.

Examples

"Mrs. Wrinkle is the devil."
"Life is one long scary roller coaster!"
