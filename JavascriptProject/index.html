Spheroid Invasion


Instructions:

Point the cannon using the mouse. Click to fire. Press to reset.
Now with controller support! Use the right joystick to aim and buttons 0 and 1 to shoot. Button 0 is B on Nintendo, X on Playstation, and A on Xbox. Button 1 is A on Nin, O on PS, and B on Xbox.
Press to go to full screen. Note: this restarts the game.
